When to use Google Business Profile

Any business where people search nearby before buying: restaurants, clinics, salons, gyms, repair, dentists and service companies. Use it when the platform behaviour matches the buying journey, not because someone in a meeting said the brand needs to be everywhere.

The Grassroots play

Build around local SEO, reviews, photos, posts, calls and map pack visibility. Start with one sharp offer, multiple creative angles and a measurement setup that can separate noise from money.

What to avoid

Ignoring reviews until there is a crisis. That is not a strategy, it is weather.

How Grassroots would start

  • Audit the offer, landing page and tracking before media spend.
  • Write five hooks based on audience pain, proof, objection, urgency and contrast.
  • Launch lean creative tests, kill weak angles quickly and move budget into winners.
  • Report on leads, revenue and sales quality, not applause metrics.
