When to use Instagram Ads

Visual brands, hospitality, beauty, fashion, events, local services and creator-led offers. Use it when the platform behaviour matches the buying journey, not because someone in a meeting said the brand needs to be everywhere.

The Grassroots play

Build around creative volume, Reels hooks, social proof and retargeting. Start with one sharp offer, multiple creative angles and a measurement setup that can separate noise from money.

Formats worth testing

  • Reels ads for attention
  • Stories for urgency
  • Carousel ads for offers and proof
  • Lead forms for enquiries

Metrics that matter

  • Cost per qualified lead
  • Hold rate on Reels
  • Profile visits
  • Booked calls or WhatsApp clicks

What to avoid

Pretty posts with no offer. Instagram rewards taste, but revenue comes from the angle.

How Grassroots would start

  • Audit the offer, landing page and tracking before media spend.
  • Write five hooks based on audience pain, proof, objection, urgency and contrast.
  • Launch lean creative tests, kill weak angles quickly and move budget into winners.
  • Report on leads, revenue and sales quality, not applause metrics.
